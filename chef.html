After graduating from Johnson and Whales culinary institute in 1999

Sal took all of his savings, and support from his family - and bought a food truck.

The monumental success of that truck afforded Sal the opportunity to open his first brick and mortar shop

In 2002, Which Bagel opened - staffed by family and friends, and still operating to this day.

Sals next big dream is to franchise the business without losing quality or atmosphere
