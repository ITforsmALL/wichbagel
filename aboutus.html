Wich Bagel is the award winning bagel sandwich shop that your friends talk about all the time!


Since 1999, Wich Bagel has won the Silver or Gold "Dough for Dough" award in each and every year.
Wich Bagel has also been featured in magazines such as "Ladles and Bagels", "Nobody Likes the Center", and "Bagel, Bagel, Bagel".
The New York Times raved "It's a Bagel so good, it's featured in the New York Times".

We pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients, and the most innovative techniques to produce an incomporable product.
All of our ingredients are sourced from local and regional growers and farms. The only frozen inventory in our shops is ice. You will never get a bagel at Wich Bagel that's more than 12 hours old.
That is our promise to you! All of our remaining inventory is donated to local charities and soup kitchens.

Owned and operated by Sal "Sam" Wich, and staffed by his family and friends - it's more than a shop - it's family.
Our original location produces more than 30,000 bagels each week, and serves our entire community.
Future plans include nation-wide sales of bagels, as well as expanded locations, and possibly frachise opportunities!

In addition to all of our unenjoyed bagels being donated to local charities, 10% of all proceeds are donated to community causes and charities across the region.
